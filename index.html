Court Guidelines

Ensuring a safe, clean, and enjoyable experience for everyone

Proper Attire

Appropriate athletic wear and clean, non-marking indoor court shoes are required. Denim, sandals, and casual footwear are not permitted on court surfaces.

Food & Beverages

Outside food and drinks are welcome in the lounge area. Only water in sealed containers is allowed on courts to protect the playing surface.

Court Etiquette

Respect scheduled bookings, maintain good sportsmanship, and be mindful of other players. Please arrive on time and vacate promptly after your session.

Facility Care

Help maintain cleanliness by disposing of trash properly. Please take all personal belongings when leaving.

Children

Children are welcome but must be supervised at all times. They may only be on courts during active play.

Prohibited Items

Pets and smoking/vaping are strictly prohibited throughout the facility for everyone's comfort and safety.
