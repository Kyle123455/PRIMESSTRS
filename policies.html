Policies & Guidelines

📋 Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Court Booking & Usage

  • All court bookings are for 1-hour time slots unless otherwise specified.
  • Players must check in at the reception upon arrival.
  • Booking times include setup and cleanup - please be respectful of the schedule.
  • Late arrivals will not receive extended time beyond the booked slot.

2. Pickleball-Specific Guidelines

  • Paddle Testing: Please test new paddles in designated practice areas only.
  • Ball Retrieval: Use proper ball retrieval techniques to avoid stepping onto adjacent courts.
  • Court Etiquette: Wait for your turn and acknowledge good plays from opponents.
  • Warm-up: Warm-up sessions should not exceed 5 minutes when others are waiting.

3. Code of Conduct

  • Attire: Proper athletic wear and non-marking shoes are required.
  • Language: Respectful language and behavior is expected at all times.
  • Safety: No running between courts during active play.

4. Facility Rules

  • Food & Drinks: Water bottles are permitted on the courts only.
  • Smoking: Smoking is strictly prohibited anywhere in the facility.
  • Pets: No pets allowed except service animals.
  • Children: Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult at all times.

💰 Refund & Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Period: All cancellation requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before your scheduled booking.

Important Notes

  • Grace Period: We hold your court for the full duration of your booking.
  • Late Arrival: Your booking end time remains the same if you arrive late.
  • Contact: If running late, please call 0976 311 5796.

How to Request Cancellation

  1. Call or text: 0976 311 5796
  2. Email: book@primesstrs.com with your booking reference
  3. Visit our facility in person

Special Circumstances

  • Medical Emergencies: With proper documentation, full refunds may be granted.
  • Facility Closure: 100% refund or priority rescheduling.
  • Weather: Covered courts, bookings proceed rain or shine.
