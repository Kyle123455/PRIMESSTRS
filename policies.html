1. Court Booking & Usage

  • All court bookings are for 1-hour time slots unless otherwise specified.
  • Players must check in at the reception upon arrival.
  • Booking times include setup and cleanup - please be respectful of the schedule.
  • Late arrivals will not receive extended time beyond the booked slot. Your court time begins at your scheduled start time regardless of arrival.

2. Pickleball-Specific Guidelines

  • Paddle Testing: Please test new paddles in designated practice areas only.
  • Ball Retrieval: Use proper ball retrieval techniques to avoid stepping onto adjacent courts.
  • Court Etiquette: Wait for your turn and acknowledge good plays from opponents.
  • Warm-up: Warm-up sessions should not exceed 5 minutes when others are waiting.
  • Scoring: Familiarize yourself with standard pickleball scoring rules before competitive play.

3. Code of Conduct

  • Attire: Proper athletic wear and shoes are required.
  • Language: Respectful language and behavior is expected at all times.
  • Safety: No running between courts during active play.

4. Facility Rules

  • Food & Drinks: Water bottles are permitted on the courts. All other food and drinks must be consumed in the designated seating area.
  • Smoking: Smoking cigarette is strictly prohibited anywhere in the facility. Vaping is permitted.
  • Pets: No pets allowed except service animals.
  • Children: Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult at all times.

Important Notes About Late Arrivals

  • Grace Period: We hold your court for the full duration of your booking..
  • After 15 minutes: Your court will not be released to other players during your booked time. If you are running late, we appreciate a courtesy call to let us know.
  • Late Arrival: If you arrive late, your booking end time remains the same (you won't get extra time).
  • Contact Us: If you're running late, please call 0976 311 5796 to let us know.

Rescheduling Policy

  • 48+ hours before: Free rescheduling to any available date/time
  • 24-48 hours before: You may reschedule your booking or receive a 50% refund.
  • Less than 24 hours before: Reschedule possible with â‚±50 admin fee and full payment for new slot

How to Request a Cancellation or Reschedule

  1. Call or text: 0976 311 5796 (fastest response)
  2. Email: book@primesstrs.com with your booking reference number
  3. Visit our facility in person at least 48 hours before your booking

Please include your booking ID and reason for cancellation to expedite the process.

Special Circumstances

  • Medical Emergencies: With proper documentation (medical certificate), full refunds may be granted even within 24 hours.
  • Facility Closure: If PRIME SSTRS cancels due to maintenance or unforeseen circumstances, you receive 100% refund or priority rescheduling.
  • Weather-Related: Since our courts are covered, bookings proceed rain or shine unless extreme weather forces facility closure.

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days and credited back to the original payment method. GCash refunds are typically instant, while card refunds may take 5-7 business days depending on your bank.

Pro Tip: If you're unsure about your schedule, consider booking during off-peak hours (weekday mornings) which have more flexibility for rescheduling. Weekend and evening bookings are in high demand and may have limited rescheduling options.
