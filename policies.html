📋 Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Court Booking

  • All bookings are for 1-hour slots
  • Check in at reception upon arrival
  • Booking includes setup & cleanup time
  • Late arrivals end at scheduled time

2. Pickleball Guidelines

  • Paddle Testing: In designated areas only
  • Ball Retrieval: Avoid stepping onto adjacent courts
  • Court Etiquette: Wait your turn, acknowledge good plays
  • Warm-up: Max 5 minutes when others waiting

3. Code of Conduct

  • Attire: Athletic wear and non-marking shoes required
  • Language: Respectful behavior expected at all times
  • Safety: No running between courts during play

4. Facility Rules

  • Food & Drinks: Water only on courts
  • Smoking: Vaping only, no cigarettes
  • Pets: Service animals only
  • Children: Must be supervised
